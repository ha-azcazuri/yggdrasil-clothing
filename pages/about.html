About us

Yggdrasil was born in 2016 as a small local business in Trondheim, Norway. Over the years, what started as a side project for Hilde Rye and Stian Tonnessen, grew out to become their main project, as they expanded to other cities in the country, and eventually out of Norway. Today, Yggdrasil ships to all around the world and keeps growing day by day.
We strive to make our clothing brand the one that everyone thinks of when it comes to Nordic related clothing. To achieve this, we work closely with the best textile factories in Norway, to make sure our products are of top quality. We have also worked along various designers on this journey, until in that way joined us Anders Birkeland as Head Designer.
Our goal is to combine the Nordic culture and folk with items that anyone in any place can enjoy, and in that way, share some of our history and keep it alive in everyone's mind, all while giving our customers the best product they could wish for.
