Here is how to get the right measures

Chest

Starting on the armpit, go around the widest part of the thorax with a taping measure.

Waist

Stand with your feet together, then measure the widest part of your waist.

Sleeve

Use a similar item that you wear as a reference. Measure from the seam of the shoulder down to the end of the sleeve.

Long

Take a similar item that you wear as a reference. Place it tightened on a flat surface and measure the total length from neck to waist.
